## Key Data Sources by Theme

### Land and Dispossession
- LARR Act (Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement) — government notifications
- SRA (Slum Rehabilitation Authority) project data — Mumbai
- Urban demolition data — housing rights organisations (Housing and Land Rights Network)
- Forest Rights Act compliance / rejection data (MoTA reports)

### Language and Erasure
- People's Linguistic Survey of India
- Census language data — mother tongue series (2011, and pending 2021)
- Endangered Languages Project
- Eighth Schedule language politics — Parliamentary debates

### Labour and Migration
- NSSO/PLFS (Periodic Labour Force Survey) migration and employment data
- CMIE CPHS household panel data
- Inter-state migrant worker reports — NHRC, NCRL

### Ecology and Extraction
- Forest Survey of India annual reports
- Mining lease data — Ministry of Mines
- River enclosure and diversion data — NWC, Interlinking of Rivers project documents
- Global Forest Watch (GFW) — deforestation tracking

### Political Violence
- UAPA arrest data — NCRB
- Caste atrocity data — NCRB
- Targeted demolition documentation — Amnesty, HRW, ACHR

## Notes on Data Ethics

Data used in this work is treated as contested record, not neutral evidence.
The act of counting, classifying, and representing carries political stakes.
All data is cited with source, date, and limitations noted.
